Future in UI Design is growing Everyday and Here is all you need to know about it.

It is no secret that the internet is one of the best places to make money. The best part is you can make money from the comfort of your own home. You can do all of this while still having a life and making time for yourself and your family.
The world is moving closer to a universal internet and it brings a lot of new changes in terms of UI, UX, etc. UI Design is also changing. What was once a static design, has become a dynamic and interactive interface. In this article, we will talk about the things which are trending now and will be in the future.

If You want to Learn about UI Designing Then you need to get in touch with these apps first :-


        Figma         Sketch

     Adobe XD       Photoshop

After this you Need to LEARN UI DESIGNS BASICS!!!

1. UI Color Theory
2. Design System
3. Icon
4. Grid System
5. Accessibility
6. Typography
7. Visual Hierarchy

After these things you need to your the most Powerful Weapon in this Stream which is Practice!!

1. Try to Replicate real Designs or Templates Designs.
2. Seek for Good Designs from the Internet and Practice to copy evry small Details.
3. Try to do Daily design Challenges (the most effective way to become an expert)
4. Try to Enhance the Default Design or Template and Get Reviews about it from Others.

Start Doing Some Personal Projects

Once you think that you are now good enough and have a good grip on the basics of UI design and are ready to replicate real apps perfectly then you can start a personal Project (Complete app/ website).

Some Ideas :

• Trip booking App
• Sleep Tracking App
• App Launcher
• Event Booking App

Never Stop Learning

No Matter how much you know or how many years of experience you have never thought you know Everything so never stops learning.
You can pick many other design tools that will help you to be more creative and unique with your designs.
